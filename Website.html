José Rizal’s Life and Legacy

A Digital Museum Linking the Past and Present of Rizal.

About

This exhibit showcases the life, work, and legacy of Dr. José Rizal. You'll discover his childhood, education, travels, writings, trials, and sacrifice through this digital timeline. This museum aims to honor Rizal as a human being molded by the environment and as a national hero whose spirit lives on in ideas.
