About

José Rizal belonged to a middle-class, landholding family with Chinese, Spanish, and Filipino ancestry. His family valued education, faith, and civic duty. Rizal’s early childhood in Calamba and Biñan, his Jesuit training at Ateneo Municipal, and his medical studies at UST and in Europe shaped him into a polymath and reformist. His life reveals how a young man’s upbringing, education, and ideals could inspire a nation’s fight for freedom.
